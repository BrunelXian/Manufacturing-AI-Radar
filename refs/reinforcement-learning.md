# Reinforcement Learning References

## Scope Of This Section

This page is for reinforcement learning papers in manufacturing, especially where sequential decision-making, uncertainty, delayed reward, or simulation-to-real transfer matter.

## Suggested Subcategories

- process control
- robotic manufacturing
- scheduling and planning
- safe reinforcement learning
- offline reinforcement learning
- simulation-backed policy learning

## How Papers Should Be Recorded

Record the manufacturing task, reward structure, environment type, training setting, and whether the work reaches real industrial relevance.
